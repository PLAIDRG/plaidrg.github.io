About Us

We are the Formal Methods and Aritificial Intelligence Interpretability Directed Reading Group (DRG) at UGA (FM+xAI). Our aim is to provide an ecosystem for studying these topics at UGA collaboratively. We investigate topics such as mechanistic interpretability, ad-hoc interpretability, formalization (especially via ITPs like Lean), type theory, compositionality, mathematical frameworks for AI, and more. We meet once a month to discuss papers from the reading list in a group seminar format.
